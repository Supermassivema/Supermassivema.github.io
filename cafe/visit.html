What to Expect When You Visit

Pull open the door and you'll find a space designed to feel like home—light-filled, warm, and welcoming. Grab a seat at the communal table, tuck into a corner booth, or perch at the bar and watch our baristas work.

We're work-friendly (wifi and power outlets available), kid-friendly (high chairs and patience), and always happy to accommodate dietary needs. Stay for ten minutes or three hours—either way, you're welcome here.

Reservations

Walk-ins always welcome, but we recommend reserving for weekend brunch or groups of 4+.
