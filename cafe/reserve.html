Reservation Policies

Timing

Tables are reserved for 90 minutes during peak times (weekends 9am-1pm). Outside peak times, stay as long as you like.

Cancellations

Life happens. If you need to cancel or reschedule, please let us know at least 2 hours in advance.

Large Groups

Groups of 8+ require advance booking and may need to select from a set menu. Contact us to discuss options.

Walk-Ins

We always hold tables for walk-ins. If you can't reserve, come by anyway—we'll do our best to seat you.
